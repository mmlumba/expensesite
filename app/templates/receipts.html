Receipts

Please note: receipts are required for any expenses over $25. Use the 'upload receipts' button to submit your receipts. You may include up to 10 PDF files and/or 10 photos of receipts.

iPad Users: You must first take photos of receipts and upload from the iPad Camera Roll/Photo Gallery. When the iPad camera is used to take a photo during upload the web form may crash. To avoid this behavior, take all photos first and then upload. Computer users, please disregard this note.

It is a best practice to Save Progress after each file upload.
